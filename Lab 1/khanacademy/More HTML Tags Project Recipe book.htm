Recipe #1

Pancakes

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 5 min
Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour 1 cup
White sugar 2 tablespoons
Baking powder 2 teaspoons
Salt 1 teaspoons
Milk 1 cup
Vegetable oil 2 tablespoons
Egg 1

Step 1: Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl. Make a well in the center, and pour in milk, oil, and egg. Mix until smooth.

Step 2: Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake; cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, 1 to 2 minutes. Flip and cook until browned on the other side. Repeat with remaining batter.

Omelette Pie

  • Time: 15 min
  • Serves: 5 min
Ingredients Quantity
Egg 6
Frozen shredded
hash brown
potatoes - thawed		 3 cups
Shredded sharp
cheddar chees		 1cup
Cooked ham - diced 1/2 cup
Bacon(cooked) 6 strips
Onion powder 1/2 teaspoons
Dried thyme 1/2 teaspoon

Step 1: Lightly grease a 9-inch pie pan. In a medium-sized cup or bowl, beat the eggs with onion powder, thyme, salt and pepper. In a large bowl, combine potatoes, cheese, ham, green pepper, bacon and the egg mixture. Pour mixture into prepared pie pan.

Step 2: Bake at 350 degrees for 40 to 45 minutes or until knife or toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

Recipe #3

Brownie

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 15 min
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 1/2 cup
White sugar 1 cup
Baking powder 1/4 teaspoons
Salt 1/4 teaspoons
Vanilla extract 1 teaspoon
All-purpose flour 1/2 cup
Egg 2
Cocoa powder 1/3 cup

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan.

Step 2: Melt 1/2 cup butter in a large saucepan. Remove from heat, and stir in sugar, eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Beat in 1/3 cup cocoa, flour, salt, and baking powder. Spread batter into prepared pan.

Step 3: Bake in the preheated oven until top is dry and edges have started to pull away from the sides of the pan, about 25 to 30 minutes. Let cool briefly before frosting.

Step 4: To make the frosting: Combine softened butter, confectioners' sugar, 3 tablespoons cocoa, honey, and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract in a bowl. Stir until smooth. Frost brownies while they are still warm.
